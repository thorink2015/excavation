## 3. Anchor text rules

Every internal link uses descriptive anchor text. Vary it across pages.

**Use:**
- "View our pool excavation services"
- "See how we work with pool builders"
- "Get a utility trenching quote"
- "Learn more about rough grading"
- "Read our excavation page"

**Never use:**
- "Click here"
- "Read more" (standalone)
- "Learn more" (standalone, with no service name)
- "Here" (as the link)
