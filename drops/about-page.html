Why We Started

Site Prep Was Broken. We Fixed Our Corner of It.

We started Groundwork to fix the part of a build that always seems to go sideways: site prep. Too many builders have stories about an excavator who ghosted them, blew the schedule, or left the site torn up for the concrete crew.

Builders shouldn't have to babysit their excavator.

That is not how we run. Every job gets a clear quote, a hard start date, and a clean handoff to the next trade. Excavation, trenching, breaking, clearing, rough grading. We do the unglamorous work that makes everything after it easier.

We are owner-operated. Dardi runs every job. When you call Groundwork, you talk to the guy on the machine, not a call center.
