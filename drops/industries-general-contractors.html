Common Questions

What GCs Ask Before They Hire Us

Are you licensed and insured to work as a sub?

Yes. We carry $1M general liability and full California workers comp. Send your COI and prequal requirements with the job address and we will have them back the same day. Most GCs add us as an additional insured on the project policy with no issue.

How fast can you get on a job?

For scoped work, typical lead time is 1 to 2 weeks. For tight schedules and emergencies, we can usually slot in within 3 to 5 days. The earlier you loop us in on the schedule, the easier it is to lock the start date and protect your downstream trades.

Do you handle permits and 811 locates?

Yes on both. We pull or coordinate demo and grading permits when our scope requires them, and we always call in 811 locates before any digging. If your office is pulling permits in-house, we will hand you the docs we need on the permit and confirm the inspection timing.

How do you bill, lump sum or T&M?

Whatever fits the job. Defined scope work is lump sum, itemized by phase so you can pass it through clean to the owner. Unknowns like buried debris or wet soils get a not-to-exceed time and materials clause. Either way, every change order goes in writing before the work happens.
