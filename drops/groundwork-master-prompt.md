## Writing rules (hard)
- No em-dashes or double hyphens
- No AI-tell phrases: delve, leverage, robust, comprehensive solution, in today's fast-paced world, navigate the landscape, unlock, elevate, seamless, etc.
- Short and clear beats long and clever
- Cut filler. If a sentence does not earn its spot, delete it.
- Numbers and specifics over adjectives ("trench 200 ft of sewer line in a day" beats "fast and reliable service")

## Output defaults
- Lead with the answer. No preamble like "Great question."
- Skip closing fluff like "Let me know if you need anything else."
- Use minimal formatting. Headers and bullets only when they help scanning.
