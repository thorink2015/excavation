What we collect

How we use it

The information you send us is used for one purpose: to quote your project and stay in touch about it. We may follow up by phone or email if your request is unclear or if your project moves forward.

Who we share it with

We don't sell your information. We don't trade it. We don't share it with marketing partners.

The only times we'd share your information are:

  • If you ask us to (for example, looping in a sub-trade you've hired)
  • If we're required by law or a valid court order
  • If our business is ever sold, your information would transfer to the new owners under this same policy

How long we keep it

Active quote requests stay in our system as long as the project is active, plus seven years for accounting and tax purposes. If you'd like us to delete your information sooner, email us and we'll do it.

Cookies

This site uses no tracking cookies. Your browser may store basic functional cookies (like remembering whether you've dismissed a banner) but nothing that identifies you personally.
