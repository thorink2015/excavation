Pool demolition specifics

Two ways to demo a pool, and your city or county dictates which one is legal:

Partial demo (cap-in-place): punch holes in the bottom, break down the upper walls, fill with rubble and clean fill. Cheaper and faster. Disclosure required on resale in most areas.

Full demo (full removal): rip the shell out entirely, haul off all rubble, backfill with clean engineered fill, compact in lifts. No disclosure issues, can build on top of it later.

If you're planning to rebuild on the footprint, do the full demo. If you're filling for landscaping only, partial is usually fine. We'll tell you which one your jurisdiction requires.
